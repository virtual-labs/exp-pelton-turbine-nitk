PELTON TURBINE - CONSTANT SPEED

Objective

To conduct performance test on Pelton wheel under constant speed and to plot the operating characteristics.

Apparatus used:

Pelton Wheel setup with Centrifugal Pump, Pressure Gauges, Spring Balance with Weights, etc.


 Description Impulse turbine: is a turbine that is driven by high velocity jets of water or steam from a nozzle directed on to vanes or buckets attached to a wheel.

Reaction turbine: is a type of turbine that develops torque by reacting to the pressure or weight of a fluid.

Gross head (Hg): It is the vertical difference between headrace and tailrace.

Net head (H): is the actual head available at the inlet of the turbine to work.
